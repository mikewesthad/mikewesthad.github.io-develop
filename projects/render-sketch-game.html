Overview

Render Sketch Game was started by Ryan Green. 3D modeling can be tedious & detail-oriented, so the idea is to borrow from gesture drawing: set a 10 minute timer and sketch until time is up.

These are a collection of my render sketches. The full site of my sketches is here. Ryan Green and Josh Larsons's tumblr of everyone's render sketches is here.
